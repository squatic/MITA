Sugar Forecasting - A Monte Carlo Simulation

Welcome everyone!

This program is created to forecast sugar price to help Sugar Farmers, Traders and Sugar Industries. I predicts the risks of Sugar Prices Displayed through graph. Also, the program is capable to predict the sugar price trending weekly/depending on users input.

How to use:
1. open the link : https://montecarlosugarapp.streamlit.app
2. prepare your CSV file with the required format display on the main page
3. Set your Model Setup. (Currently, configured on its finest setup)
4. upload CSV File
5. Click Apply GBM Parameters
6. Simulation Setting are setup on its default. You can change it according to your requirements.
7. Weekly Sugar Price Prediction Settings
  - Setup number of weeks to forecast
  - bar Graph you want to show (Median/Mean)
  - Select your Confidence interval
8. Click. Run Simulation
9. On the Main Page, (you can see under the "LSC Sugar Price Monte Carlo Risk Model) 
  - Click the Monte Carlo Simulation to view the 	Price Distribution Horizon and Perscentage 	of Risk probability of finishing at or 	below 2000/LKG.
  - Click the Weekly Price Prediction to view Bar graph(Mean/Median) according to your Criteria Input.

Enjoy....
